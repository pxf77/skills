# Portrait Prompt Examples

Use these examples as structure and tone references. Adapt the subject and styling to the user's request.

## Urban Night Emotional Portrait

**中文策划:** 都市夜景中的情绪肖像，人物安静、疏离，背景有轻微霓虹和雨后反光，画面像电影停格。

**English Prompt:** Create a realistic half-body cinematic portrait of a young adult standing on a rain-soaked city street at night. The subject wears a dark tailored coat, holds a calm introspective expression, and looks slightly away from the camera. Neon signs and wet pavement create soft reflections behind them. The image is lit with cool blue ambient light and a subtle magenta rim light, photographed with an 85mm portrait lens, shallow depth of field, and a composed film-still frame. The color grade is moody, high contrast, and realistic, with natural skin texture and gentle cinematic grain. Avoid distorted hands, plastic-looking skin, cluttered background details, and inconsistent neon lighting.

**Variants:** warmer streetlamp key light; tighter close-up crop; direct gaze; black-and-white noir finish.

## Natural-Light Film Photoshoot

**中文策划:** 自然光生活感写真，人物放松、真实，室内窗光、浅色衣物、轻微胶片颗粒。

**English Prompt:** Create a realistic natural-light portrait of a young adult sitting beside a large apartment window in the late morning. The subject wears a soft white shirt, relaxed hair, and minimal makeup, with a quiet gentle expression. Soft daylight wraps across the face, the background is a simple lived-in room with muted neutral colors, and the composition feels intimate and documentary. Photograph it with a 50mm lens, shallow depth of field, natural skin texture, subtle film grain, and a warm pastel color grade. Avoid over-smoothed skin, artificial glamour retouching, warped fingers, and messy background objects.

**Variants:** standing near the curtain; closer head-and-shoulders framing; cooler overcast daylight; faded 35mm film texture.

## Studio High-Fashion Editorial

**中文策划:** 棚拍高级时装大片，人物姿态克制、有力量，服装结构明确，背景简洁，光线精准。

**English Prompt:** Create a polished studio fashion editorial portrait of a confident adult model wearing a structured black suit and minimal silver jewelry. The subject stands in a precise three-quarter pose with a direct composed gaze. Use a clean gray studio background, a large softbox key light from the front-left, subtle fill, and a crisp rim light to define the silhouette. Photograph it with an 85mm lens, controlled depth of field, sharp editorial detail, realistic skin texture, and a refined magazine-style finish. Avoid exaggerated anatomy, waxy skin, harsh blown highlights, and distracting props.

**Variants:** white cyclorama background; stronger hard-flash editorial look; seated pose; monochrome high-contrast finish.

## Classical Painterly Portrait

**中文策划:** 古典油画感肖像，人物安静、端庄，柔和侧光，深色背景，色彩沉稳。

**English Prompt:** Create a realistic fine-art portrait of an elegant adult seated against a dark muted background. The subject wears a deep green velvet jacket, keeps a serene expression, and turns slightly toward a soft side light. The lighting should feel painterly and sculptural, with gentle shadow transitions across the face and hands. Compose the portrait as a three-quarter seated frame, photographed with classic portrait depth and a restrained palette of olive, umber, and warm ivory. The finish should resemble a refined photographic portrait with subtle painterly atmosphere, not an illustration. Avoid plastic skin texture, overdone fantasy styling, distorted hands, and muddy facial details.

**Variants:** warmer candlelit mood; closer face crop; darker low-key background; softer romantic color palette.
